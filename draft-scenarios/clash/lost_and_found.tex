\addscenariosection{1}{Clash Scenario}{Lost and Found}{\images/logistics.png}

\begin{multicols*}{2}

\textbf{Author:} Laamakala

\textit{WWith kingdoms weakened and armies scattered, explorers, mercenaries, and warlords scour the ruined lands for lost wealth and the power to rebuild their realm.}

\subsection*{\MakeUppercase{Scenario Length}}
This Scenario plays out over 10 Rounds.

\subsection*{\MakeUppercase{Player Setup}}
\textbf{Player Count:} 2-4

\textbf{Starting Resources:} 10 \svg{gold}, 0 \svg{building_materials}, 0 \svg{valuables}

\textbf{Starting Income:} 10 \svg{gold}, 0 \svg{building_materials}, 0 \svg{valuables}

\textbf{Starting Units:}
\begin{itemize}
  \item A ``Pack'' of cheapest \svg{bronze} Units with 2 × a chosen Stack Token
  \item A ``Pack'' of the cheapest \svg{silver} Units
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Town Buildings:}
\begin{itemize}
  \item Mage Guild
  \item \svg{bronze} Dwelling
\end{itemize}

\subsection*{\MakeUppercase{Map Setup}}
Take the following Map Tiles and arrange them as shown in the Scenario map layout ($P$ stands for the number of players):

\begin{itemize}
  \item $P$ × Starting (I) Map Tiles
  \item 3$P$ × Far (II--III) Map Tiles
  \item $P$ × Near (IV--V) Map Tiles (with Obelisk)
  \item 6 × Near (IV--V) Map Tiles (without Obelisk)
  \item 1 × Center (VI--VII) Map Tile
\end{itemize}

\subsection*{\MakeUppercase{Victory Condition}}
The game ends when any of the following conditions are met:
\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{4 players:} One player has defeated every opponent’s Main Hero at least once. \textit{That player wins the game immediately.}
  \item At the end of Round 10, the player controlling the Level VII Field on the Center Map Tile wins the game.
If no player controls it, the player with the most Obelisks visited wins instead. In case of a tie, the tied players draw to their \svg{hand} Limit and engage in a final Combat. The winner of that Combat wins the game.
\end{itemize}

\subsection*{\MakeUppercase{Timed Events}}
\textbf{\nth{1} -- \nth{2} Round:}
\begin{itemize}
  \item All Secondary Heroes gain +1 \svg{movement}.
\end{itemize}
\textbf{\nth{3}, \nth{6} and \nth{8} Round:}
\begin{itemize}
  \item Remove all Black Cubes from the Map except from Learning Stones.
\end{itemize}

\subsection*{\MakeUppercase{Additional Rules}}
\begin{itemize}
  \item All players start with only their Secondary Hero on an empty Field on a Far Map Tile marked ``S'' that contains a Settlement.
  \item Secondary Heroes may gain all rewards twice when visiting a Field until their Starting Town has been captured.
  \item Your M\&M Deck acts as your Secondary Hero's Hand. Discarded Cards remain in the Discard Pile until you capture your Starting Town. After that, use the M\&M Deck normally with your Main Hero.
  \item Secondary Heroes may discover Map Tiles without spending a \svg{movement} once per Round.
  \item After winning a Neutral Combat with your Secondary Hero, you may Recruit one defeated Neutral Unit for free.
  \item All Starting Tiles start undiscovered.
  \item Players may not use Buildings or Recruit Units from their Starting Town until they capture it.
  \item \textbf{Starting Towns:} Guarded by:
  \begin{itemize}
    \item 2 × A ``Pack'' of the cheapest \svg{bronze} Units
    \item A ``few'' of the most expensive \svg{silver} Units
  \end{itemize}
  \item After capturing their Starting Town, that player immediately gains their Main Hero. Draw Cards to your \svg{hand} Limit and gain Levels equal to the Round number \textit{(max 4)}.
  \item You may build the \svg{building_special_tent} Building with a discount of 2 \svg{gold} and 2 \svg{building_materials}.
\end{itemize}
