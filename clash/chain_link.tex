\addscenariosection{1}{Clash Scenario}{Chain Link}{\images/shackles_of_war.png}

\begin{multicols*}{2}

\textbf{Author:} NeuroN

\textit{Power struggle at the border escalates. This trade route is incredibly important as a supply line, and you cannot allow it to fall into enemy hands.}

\subsection*{\MakeUppercase{Scenario Length}}
12 Rounds

\subsection*{\MakeUppercase{Player Setup}}
\textbf{Player Count:} 2

\textbf{Starting Resources:} 20 \svg{gold}, 6 \svg{building_materials}, 1 \svg{valuables}

\textbf{Starting Income:} 10 \svg{gold}, 0 \svg{building_materials}, 0 \svg{valuables}

\textbf{Starting Units:}

\begin{itemize}
  \item A ``Pack'' of the cheapest \svg{bronze} Units
  \item A ``Few'' of the most expensive \svg{bronze} Units
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Town Buildings:} \svg{bronze} Dwelling

\subsection*{\MakeUppercase{Map Setup}}

Take the following Map Tiles and arrange them as shown in the Scenario map layout:

\begin{itemize}
  \item 2 × Starting (I) Map Tiles
  \item 4 × Far (II--III) Map Tiles, 2 of which must contain a Settlement
  \item 4 × Near (IV--V) Map Tiles: 2 with an Obelisk, 2 with a Settlement
  \item 1 × Center (VI--VII) Map Tile with a Grail
\end{itemize}

Place Far and Near tiles symmetrically, so that Obelisks and Settlements aren't adjacent.

\subsection*{\MakeUppercase{Victory Conditions}}
Take control of the enemy Town.

\subsection*{\MakeUppercase{Defeat Conditions}}
At end of Round 12 the game ends in a draw.

\subsection*{\MakeUppercase{Timed Events}}
\textbf{\nth{2}, \nth{6}, and \nth{10} Round:}
\begin{itemize}
  \item The \nth{2} player gains +1 \svg{movement}
\end{itemize}

\subsection*{\MakeUppercase{Additional Rules}}
\begin{itemize}
  \item At the start of each Resource round, choose either extra 1~\svg{valuables} or 2~\svg{building_materials}.
  \item You don't need to pay \svg{gold} to defend your Town and can use cards during siege.
  \item Once your Main Hero reaches Level 5, all your Heroes gain +1~\svg{movement} for every Turn.
  \item When visiting an Obelisk, choose one:
    \begin{itemize}
      \item Remove a card from your hand
      \item Gain \svg{morale_positive}
      \item Gain 1~\svg{movement}
    \end{itemize}
  \item After winning a combat on the Grail field, you may recruit one of the units you just defeated for free.
\end{itemize}
